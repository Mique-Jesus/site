01. About Me

Hey — I’m Miguel. I transform messy data into decisions that truly make a difference. For over 8 years, I’ve designed and conducted impact evaluations, social research, and analytics for national and international organisations, primarily focusing on development, anti-corruption, and justice. In addition, I'm currently exploring the challenges and implications of governments' adoption of AI.

I feel most comfortable when measurement and action come together: leading MERL processes, blending quantitative and qualitative methods, and translating models into plain language for policymakers. From building evaluation systems to shipping tailored dashboards and reports, my compass is simple — measure what matters, then make it usable.
